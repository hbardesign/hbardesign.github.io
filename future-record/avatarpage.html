Filters through Snapchat
Snapchat is primarily used for creating multimedia messages referred to as "snaps"; snaps can consist of a photo or a short video, and can be edited to include filters and effects, text captions, and drawings.
we use cameras to make the memories we experience last longer, and further capture the true world around us. Although this meaning has began to change with the rise of techology, particularly multimedia which have the power to alter the way we see the world such as snapchat. We are stimulated by these entertaining and hyper-realistic filters to change the way we look and percieve, stripping away the truth behind this fake mask. This is only the begining of this evoling technology.
